## Status Effects

### Slow ###
Reduces horizontal movement speed of the affected player. Slowed players also cannot dash.

### Stun ###
A stunned player cannot act in any way until the effect expires. Being stunned will de-activate charging and blocking.

### Bleed ###
Bleed makes the target take extra damage equal to +*10*% for each stack. Bleed stacks up to *5* times, and lasts *60* seconds from the time of the first stack. When one stack expires, they all expire.


## Activated Effects

### Critical ###
A chance to deal damage equal to *1.5*x the player's Maximum damage.

### Burn ###
Burn deals extra damage over time based on the damage of the last attack the target received.
