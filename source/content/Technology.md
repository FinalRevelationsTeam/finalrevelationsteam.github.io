
# Implants
Implants are swappable modules with varying effects that can be installed into any player with the "Cyborg" trait.


## Head
### Precognition Implant
> [!skill] Precognition Implant
> *5* Wounds
> *5* Stamina
> ###### Flat +*15*% chance to dodge any damage

### Accuracy Implant
> [!skill] Accuracy Implant
> *10* Wounds
> *20* Stamina
> ###### *35*% chance to re-roll a missed attack


## Torso
### Power Up Implant
> [!skill] Power Up Implant
> *10* Fatigue
> ###### Each tier's drain acts as if it's 1 tier lower (T1 is drainless, T2 has T1 drain, etc.)

### Bulwark Implant
> [!skill] Bulwark Implant
> *10* Wounds
> ###### For the first 10 seconds of each round, you cannot take damage and you don't lose energy.

### Stamina Implant
> [!skill] Stamina Implant
> *10* Stamina
> ###### All stamina drain is reduced by 25%.


## Arms
##### (2 Slots)
### Bleed Implant
> [!skill] Bleed Implant
> *5* Stamina
> ###### *10*% chance to inflict Bleed on hit

### Critical Implant
> [!skill] Critical Implant
> *5* Stamina
> ###### *8*% flat Critical Chance

### Reach Implant
> [!skill] Reach Implant
> *5* Stamina
> ###### Basic attacks gain *4 pixels* of extra reach


## Legs
##### (2 Slots)
### Dash Implant
> [!skill] Dash Implant
> *5* Stamina
> ###### Dashes cost *30%* less stamina

### Jump Implant
> [!skill] Jump Implant
> *5* Stamina
> ###### Grants the ability to double jump

### Reach Implant
> [!skill] Reach Implant
> *5* Stamina
> ###### Basic attacks gain *4 pixels* of extra reach


## Operating System
##### (Android Only)

### Glass Cannon Implant
> [!skill] Glass Cannon Implant
> *20* Stamina
> *10* Wounds
> ###### Strength & Force are multiplied by Durability/Resistance mods; Durability & Resistance are multiplied by Strength/Force mods

### Anger Implant
> [!skill] Anger Implant
> *20* Stamina
> *10* Wounds
> ###### Sets Anger mod to *2×* but halves Recovery

### Potency Implant
> [!skill] Potency Implant
> *20* Stamina
> *10* Wounds
> ###### Strength & Force mods always match; buffs to one affect both. Ki Fist ratio becomes *75/75*

### Projectile Absorption Implant
> [!skill] Projectile Absorption Implant
> *30* Stamina
> *5* Wounds
> *5* Fatigue
> ###### Nullifies projectile damage; each % of prevented projectile damage grants *5 stamina*
